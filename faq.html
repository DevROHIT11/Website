Frequently Asked Questions

What is STES Robotics? ▼
STES Robotics is a team of passionate innovators from Sinhgad Institutes focused on creating and building robots to solve real-world problems.
How can I join STES Robotics? ▼
You can join by applying through our "Apply Now" section or attending our recruitment drives held annually.
What competitions does the team participate in? ▼
We participate in national and international competitions such as Robocon, Techfest, and various hackathons.
Do I need prior experience to join? ▼
No prior experience is required. We welcome enthusiastic learners and provide training to new members.
What resources does the team use? ▼
We use tools like CAD software, microcontrollers, 3D printers, and various robotics kits to develop our projects.
Who can apply to join STES Robotics? ▼
Any student from Sinhgad Institutes who is passionate about robotics and technology can apply.
How do I stay updated with team activities? ▼
Follow us on social media platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube for the latest updates.
Can I collaborate with STES Robotics? ▼
Yes, we are open to collaborations with other teams, industries, and organizations to work on innovative projects.
